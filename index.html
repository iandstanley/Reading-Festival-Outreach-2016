Reading Outreach

We are a group of born again Christians who live in Reading, Berkshire who believe in the Great Commission to spread the gospel whereever we find ourselves.

We can be reguarly found on the streets of Reading doing outreach to all who would listen

Reading Festival 2016 Outreach

We are joining forces again to reach out to the 80,000 people who descend on Reading each year for the Reading Festival. Together we are setting up a programme of outreach where we hope to see dozens if not hundreds turn to Jesus this year. Please see the events listed below for where and when we are out.

Churches Involved

The events listed here are run by members from the following churches:

  • Carey Baptist Church
  • Lifespring
  • Network Vineyard
  • Reading Family Church
  • The Gate

    • And also from a programme called The Turning which is a smaller group of Christians from The Gate, Reading Family Church and Vineyard who run regular soaking evenings and outreach training in Reading
